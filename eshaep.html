About Me

My name is Brian Lee and I am a 2023 alumnus graduated from Virginia Tech with a Bachelor's of Science in Computational Modeling and Data Analytics (CMDA) and minors in computer science and mathematics. My interests lie in the use of data science techniques in physical science problems, including bioinformatics and physics. In addition, I am interested in applying data science techniques to develop the understanding on machine learning methods to become safe, effective, and efficient tools of the future.

My experience consists of data visualization, data analysis of complex high dimensional datasets, and the design of software tools that aid the research process such as data pipelines or visualization modules. My coursework is diverse and interdisciplinary in computer science, mathematics, and statistics. My cirriculum favors courses focused on math modeling methods in dynamical systems, matrix theory, and modeling solutions to partial differential equations.
